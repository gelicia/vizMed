Visualizing Hospital Price Data

This visualization allows you to explore the charges that providers submit to Medicare.

The costs used for the bar chart are the average cost per discharge for that state/city/provider. The average and max cost for all the bars displayed on the page is an average of those values. Go here for more information about the calculation.

Click on a bar to drill down into that state/city/provider.
